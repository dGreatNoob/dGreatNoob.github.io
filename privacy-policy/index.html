Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Belteshazzar Marquez (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information in connection with (1) our AI chatbot service for Facebook Messenger and Facebook Page inquiries used by businesses (“Chatbot”), and (2) this website. By using the Chatbot or this website, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

Through the Chatbot (Facebook Messenger and Page inquiries)

When you interact with our AI chatbot on Facebook Messenger or through a Facebook Page that uses our service, we may collect and process:

  • Messages and conversation content – What you send to the Chatbot (questions, comments, or other text) so we can generate replies and support the business you are contacting.
  • Information provided by Facebook – Data we receive through Meta’s APIs in accordance with the permissions granted by the Page or app, which may include: your Facebook name or Page-scoped user ID, profile picture (if provided by the API), and other identifiers or metadata necessary to deliver and operate the Chatbot (e.g., conversation and message IDs). We do not collect more data than is needed to provide the service.
  • Usage and technical data – Logs of interactions (e.g., timestamps, message types) to operate, debug, and improve the Chatbot and to comply with our agreements with businesses and with Meta.

Through this website

This website is a static portfolio site. We may collect:

  • Usage data – If we use analytics, we may collect information such as approximate location, device type, and pages viewed to understand how the site is used.
  • Information you send voluntarily – If you contact us via email or a contact form, we use the information you provide (e.g., name, email, message) only to respond to you and not for marketing unless you agree.

2. How We Use the Information

  • To provide the Chatbot – We use your messages and relevant data from Facebook to generate AI-powered responses, answer inquiries on behalf of the business, and maintain conversation context within a session.
  • To improve our services – We may use aggregated or anonymized data to improve the Chatbot’s accuracy, train or tune models (where permitted), and fix issues.
  • To operate and secure our systems – We use data as needed for hosting, security, and compliance with our obligations to Meta and the businesses that use the Chatbot.
  • For this website – We use website data only to run and improve the site and to respond to your communications.

3. How We Process and Store Data

Your messages and related data may be processed by automated systems, including third-party AI or cloud services we use to power the Chatbot. Data is stored on secure servers and retained only as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with law, or enforce our agreements. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Sharing

We may share data:

  • With the business – The business whose Page or Messenger uses our Chatbot may receive conversation content or summaries to respond to inquiries and run their operations.
  • With Meta / Facebook – Data is shared with Meta as required to operate on the Facebook/Messenger platform and in line with the Meta Privacy Policy and our agreements with Meta.
  • With service providers – We may use third-party providers for hosting, AI processing, and analytics. They process data on our instructions and under agreements that protect your data.

5. How to Request Deletion of Your Data

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time. To do so:

  • Email us – Send a clear request to mbelteshazzar.bm.1247@gmail.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request” and include your name and, if possible, the Facebook name or Page you used when contacting the Chatbot. We will process your request within a reasonable time and confirm when deletion is complete.
  • Contact the business – For conversations that took place on a specific business’s Page or Messenger, you may also contact that business directly; they may have their own process for handling deletion requests.

We will delete or anonymize your data except where we must retain it for legal, security, or legitimate business purposes.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, restrict, or delete your personal data, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, use the contact details in Section 5 or Section 10. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your country.

7. Cookies and Local Storage (Website)

This website may use local storage (e.g., to remember a theme preference). Such data stays on your device. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies on this site.

8. Third-Party Links and Services

This website may link to third-party sites (e.g., GitHub, LinkedIn). Those sites have their own privacy policies. Our Chatbot integrates with Facebook/Meta; their practices are described in Meta’s policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties.

9. Hosting and Infrastructure

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. The Chatbot may be hosted on third-party cloud or AI platforms. Those providers may collect and process data as described in their respective privacy statements.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when changes are made. Continued use of the Chatbot or this website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, your data, or to submit a data deletion request, contact us at mbelteshazzar.bm.1247@gmail.com.
